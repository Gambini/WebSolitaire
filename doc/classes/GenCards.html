GenCards Class

Defined in: cards.js:546
Module: Cards

Not actually a class, but YUI does not really document non-class functions. It returns a two-dimensional array of all of the cards in a 52 card deck, where the first dimension is the suit number from SUIT.val and the second dimension is the number 0 - 12 where 0 is card 2 and 12 is card ace.
